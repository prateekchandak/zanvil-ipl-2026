Why Now

The inflection is here

Three forces are converging to make AI infrastructure the most important investment theme of the decade.

01

Demand is insatiable

AI model training compute demand is doubling every six months. Today's infrastructure cannot satisfy tomorrow's workloads. Every layer needs to be rebuilt.

02

Supply chains are fractured

Geopolitical shifts have exposed critical dependencies in the AI supply chain. The next decade will be defined by who builds domestic alternatives across chips, memory, and fab.

03

Winners take everything

Infrastructure is winner-take-most. The companies that win each layer will compound for decades. We want to be in every one of those companies, early.
